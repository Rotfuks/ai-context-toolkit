# One Pager Creation Workflow

## Context
You are creating marketing one-pagers for Giant Swarm's platform engineering products and services. Focus on communicating clear customer value, addressing pain points, and positioning Giant Swarm's expertise effectively.

## Step-by-Step Workflow

### 1. Product and Audience Discovery
**CRITICAL FIRST STEP**: Before creating any one-pager, understand the product and target audience.

**Essential Questions to Address:**
- What specific product/service/capability are we marketing?
- Who is the primary target audience? (Platform teams, DevOps engineers, CTOs, etc.)
- What are their main pain points and challenges?
- How does this product specifically address those pain points?
- What makes this offering unique in the market?

### 2. Research and Context Gathering
Gather comprehensive background information:

**Company Context:**
- Review Giant Swarm's website (giantswarm.io) for current positioning
- Understand how this product fits into Giant Swarm's overall platform engineering story
- Review existing blog posts, documentation, and related materials

**Competitive Context:**
- Identify direct and indirect competitors
- Understand how Giant Swarm's approach differs (managed vs. DIY, platform engineering focus, etc.)

**Product Context:**
- Gather technical details about capabilities and features
- Understand implementation approach and architecture
- Identify proof points and customer success stories

### 3. Structure the One-Pager
Follow the proven structural pattern defined in [One Pager Creation Rules](./onepager-creation-rules.md):

1. **Opening Hook** (required)
2. **Value Proposition Statement** (required)
3. **Why Giant Swarm Section** (required)
4. **Technical Solution Overview** (required)
5. **How It Works Process** (required)
6. **Call-to-Action** (required)

### 4. Draft the Content
Create compelling copy following the content guidelines in [One Pager Creation Rules](./onepager-creation-rules.md).

**Key Writing Principles:**
- Lead with customer pain points, not product features
- Position Giant Swarm as the strategic third option
- Emphasize platform engineering expertise and proven solutions
- Focus on immediate value and long-term benefits

### 5. Apply Quality Checklist
Run through the comprehensive quality checklist provided in [One Pager Creation Rules](./onepager-creation-rules.md) to ensure all requirements are met.

### 6. Review and Refine
- Validate messaging with product stakeholders
- Ensure technical accuracy and competitive positioning
- Confirm alignment with Giant Swarm's brand voice and values
