## Example 3

Coming straight from a Hashtag#FoundationSprint with Giant Swarm to find a testable Hashtag#AI value hypothesis I had the pleasure to jump right into the WeAreDevelopers Hashtag#WorldCongress2025. And, well, still being in a somewhat "Search-For-AI-Value" mindset was just perfect, because AI was definitely the big topic in this year's edition of the amazing Berlin based conference. 

This year I definitely couldn't get enough of the great panel discussions on the Hashtag#TechLeaders Stage! Whenever I looked up my next bookmarked session, it was at the TechLeaders Stage again, that says a lot! :D

My highlights? Definitely the panel session, where Mirko Novakovic from Dash0, Jan Oberhauser from n8n, and Alex Laubscher from Windsurf shared insights into how AI is impacting engineering orgs. According to their discussion there is not yet any big 'disruptive' change in organisational structures due to AI, since most engineering orgs are still in an exploration phase with the tools and new opportunities. Funny enough the biggest impact so far is on the application process because now everyone has perfectly refined CVs and no trouble with any async remote hiring tasks :)

Another highlight was the panel discussion from Robin Hermann from STACKIT, Boris Hecker from Eviden/Atos, and Clemens Wasner from EnliteAI about AI sovereignty - or rather about the Hashtag#europe's place and opportunities in the AI race. One important message of the discussion, which resonated with me was, that europes biggest chance is the focus on Hashtag#OpenSource and becoming the place to be for Hashtag#OSS founders and companies. 
But one of the most important statements imo came from Robin, when he stated, that a lot of people in europe and especially germany tend to focus a lot on negatives and failures - but in truth there're also plenty of success stories and great organizations doing amazing work. 

I can only support that optimism, especially after all these great insights and stories from the Hashtag#WeAreDevelopers World Congress 2025. I'm sure we'll see lots more about these amazing stories in the future!
