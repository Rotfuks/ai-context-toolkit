## Quality Checkpoints

### Before Publishing
1. **Authenticity Check**: Does this sound like Dominik's genuine voice?
2. **Value Verification**: Will readers learn something or be inspired?
3. **Community Focus**: Does it build connections or start conversations?
4. **Technical Accuracy**: Are all technical claims correct and current?
5. **Attribution Complete**: Are all people and sources properly credited?

### Red Flags to Avoid
- **Generic Business Speak**: Avoid corporate jargon without personality
- **Pure Self-Promotion**: Always provide value beyond personal/company promotion
- **Buzzword Overload**: Balance technical terms with accessible language
- **Negative Industry Commentary**: Stay constructive and solution-focused
- **Overly Formal Tone**: Maintain conversational, approachable style
